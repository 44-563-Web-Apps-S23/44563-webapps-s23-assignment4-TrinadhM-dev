Trinadh Machanavazzala "snips a play"

MANIKIN AND MINIKIN(A BISQUE-PLAY) BY ALFRED KREYMBORG

Manikin
. Well, I'd venture to say, if she'd only stay away some morning----
Minikin
. That's what I say in my dreams!
Manikin
. She and her broom----
Minikin
. Her everlasting broom----
Manikin
. She wouldn't be sweeping----
Minikin
. Every corner, every cranny, every crevice----
Manikin
. And the dust wouldn't move----
Minikin
. Wouldn't crawl, wouldn't rise, wouldn't fly----
Manikin
. And cover us all over----
Minikin
. Like a spider-web--ugh!
Manikin
. Everlasting dust has been most of our life----
Minikin
. Everlasting years and years of dust!
Manikin
. You on your lovely blue gown----
Minikin
. And you on your manly pink cloak.
Manikin
. If she didn't sweep, we wouldn't need dusting----
Minikin
. Nor need taking down, I should say----
Manikin
. With her stupid, clumsy hands----
Minikin
. Her crooked, monkey paws----
