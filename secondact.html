Manikin.

Well, I'd venture to say, if she'd only stay away some morning----

Minikin.

That's what I say in my dreams!

Manikin.

She and her broom----

Minikin.

Her everlasting broom----

Manikin.

She wouldn't be sweeping----

Minikin.

Every corner, every cranny, every crevice----

Minikin.

[_The clock goes on ticking for a moment. Its mellow chimes strike the hour._]
